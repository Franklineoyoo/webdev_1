About Me

Hello, I'm Frankline Oyoo.
A Web Designer, Software Developer, and AI Enthusiast passionate about building scalable, high-performance digital solutions. With a strong foundation in Software Development from Moringa School and Power Learn Project Academy, alongside AI expertise from ALX Africa, I specialize in developing user-centric applications that enhance business operations and customer engagement.

I specialize in full-stack development, leveraging HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, and Python to create scalable and high-performance software solutions. I also have experience with Java and Dart for mobile and backend development. With strong expertise in database management using MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, I ensure seamless data integration, optimized performance, and secure data handling.

On the front end, I work with frameworks and libraries such as React.js, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS to build responsive and visually appealing user interfaces. On the backend, I utilize Node.js with Express.js, Flask, and Django to develop robust and efficient server-side applications.

My experience in mobile app development with Flutter and React Native allows me to create cross-platform applications that provide a smooth and engaging user experience. Additionally, I specialize in API integration to facilitate seamless communication between digital systems and enhance application functionality.

Beyond development, I have a strong background in DevOps and version control, working with Git, GitHub, CI/CD pipelines, and Docker to streamline deployment and collaboration. My expertise extends to WordPress development using Elementor, allowing me to design and optimize websites for diverse business needs.

With hands-on experience in customer support and CRM systems such as Zendesk, Salesforce, and Freshdesk, I build digital solutions that prioritize user needs, optimize workflows, and enhance customer satisfaction.

I thrive in collaborative and innovative environments, constantly refining my problem-solving, communication, and leadership skills. I stay ahead of industry trends and emerging technologies to integrate cutting-edge advancements into my projects. Whether it's developing responsive websites, crafting custom software, or building intelligent applications, I bring a proactive, detail-oriented, and results-driven approach to every project.

My mission is to leverage technology to drive business growth, improve efficiency, and create seamless user experiences. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new programming languages, contributing to open-source projects, and engaging with the tech community to learn and share knowledge.
